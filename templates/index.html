1. The Isle Of Etherise

The Isle Of Etherise is an action survival RPG game where you need to gain attribute points by killing enemies, bosses, and other players.
As you level up, you can unlock more spells or allocate points to your attributes to become more intelligent, powerful, and charismatic!
Have a great time playing with your friends and save your progress to continue playing at another time.
